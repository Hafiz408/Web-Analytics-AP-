About AshishoGram


History of AshishoGram Group

Founded back in 1987, Arun Fabricators, the first partnership of the group has been involved in Mechanical Engineering contracts mostly in the Oil & Gas Industry in India. A long line of landmark achievements thereafter have made AshishoGram Group what it is today – a leading company in the Mechanical, civil, pharma, chemical, real estate, hospitality & Infrastructure space.

Company milestones

Over the years, AshishoGram Group has gone from strength to strength, pioneering in Mechanical & civil engineering contracts for Oil majoring and creating affordable realty projects in Chennai and other parts of India.
